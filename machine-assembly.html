Precision Mechanical Sub-Assembly & Welding

Our Sub-Assembly & Welding division provides comprehensive system integration solutions for high-volume automotive mechanical sub-assemblies. Leveraging robotic welding cells and automated torque-management stations, we ensure every unit meets identical functional performance standards.

We specialize in chassis modules, engine-room components, and multi-point structural assemblies. Every assembly line is balanced using Poka-Yoke methodology and verified through 100% end-of-line functional testing.
