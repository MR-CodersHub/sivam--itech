Automated Press Shop
Infrastructure

The Automated Press Shop at Sivam Itech is a high-performance manufacturing unit dedicated to delivering precision-formed components at scale. Engineered for structural integrity and high-speed series production, this facility supports diverse automotive and industrial requirements.

Our facility utilizes a comprehensive range of stamping technology, spanning from 50T to 200T high-precision presses. This infrastructure is fully integrated with automated feeding and handling systems, ensuring optimized material utilization and continuous output efficiency.
