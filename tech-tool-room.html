Engineering Core

Foundation of Tooling Excellence

The Tech Tool Room is the R&D nucleus of Sivam Itech. By integrating high-end CAD/CAM software with high-speed 5-axis vertical machining and wire EDM technology, we design and produce tools that define the precision of every stamped component.

We specialize in complex multi-stage progressive dies and high-precision transfer tools. From initial modeling to final micron-level metrology, our workflows ensure structural die longevity and zero-defect output for Tier-1 automotive requirements.

Engineering Capabilities
