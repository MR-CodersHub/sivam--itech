Technical FAQ

Common queries from procurement and engineering teams.

What materials are routinely stamped for brake backing plates?

We primarily process high-grade carbon steels, IS 2062, SAPH 440, and C-45 parameters strictly matched against mill test certificates.

What precise geometric tolerances can the press shop hold?

Our progressive tooling structures routinely hold surface flatness down to 0.05 mm and linear positional tolerances capable of satisfying Cpk > 1.33 expectations.

Do you undertake custom stamping solutions and die development?

Yes. Our Tooling & NPD division develops 100% bespoke compound and progressive dies internally from client 3D CAD data.
