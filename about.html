About SIVAM ITECH

Reliable High-Precision Manufacturing

SIVAM ITECH Metal Forming Industries is an automotive-focused precision manufacturing company established on 5th July 2018 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. We support the automotive industry with reliable, high-precision sheet metal components and assemblies, manufactured through disciplined processes and robust quality systems.

Backed by strong technical leadership and sound financial stability, we continue to invest in infrastructure, tooling, and process automation to meet evolving customer requirements and industry standards.

Our Vision

Preferred & Trusted Automotive Supplier
